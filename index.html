Songs for the Trumpocalypse is a major, super classy book that's going to MAKE POETRY GREAT AGAIN, computationally generated from genuine Donald Trump quotations with a recurrent neural network. Created for the 2016 Stupid Shit No One Needs and Terrible Ideas Hackathon.

President Trump should consider this book my formal application for the position of United States Poet Laureate under his administration.
